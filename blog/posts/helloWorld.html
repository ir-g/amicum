How this blog works
This was entirely created by me, Deep Sohelia both the template and the post loading system. The only exception being the use of AHAH, or Asyncronous HTML and HTTP, a small (8 lines or so) script which is used to fetch these posts. There is no jQuery involved, thankfully. The system works by storing the file names of all posts that are to be loaded in an array, and then looping through the array, loading in each post. While not the most elegant of solutions, it is the lightest in terms of SLOC. It means I can use as much fancy markup as I want, making it my far the most lightweight and customizable way for me to make blog posts.
